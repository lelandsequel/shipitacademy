Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 28, 2026

Operator Foundry ("we," "our," or "us") operates ShipItAcademy.com. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Application data: Name, email address, and answers you submit through the apply form
  • Payment data: Processed securely by Stripe — we never store your card details
  • Usage data: Standard web analytics (page views, referral source) via anonymous tracking

2. How We Use It

  • To review your application and communicate about enrollment
  • To process payment and send receipts
  • To send course materials, schedules, and updates
  • To improve our programs based on aggregate, anonymous data

3. We Don't Sell Your Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. Ever.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Stripe — payment processing (stripe.com/privacy)
  • Typeform — application form (typeform.com/help/a/gdpr)
  • Vercel — website hosting (vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy)

5. Data Retention

We retain application and enrollment data for 2 years after your last interaction with us. Payment records are retained as required by law.

6. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by emailing us at leland@cl-strategy.com.

7. Cookies

We use minimal cookies for site functionality and anonymous analytics. No cross-site tracking cookies are used.

8. Contact

Questions? Email leland@cl-strategy.com
