Module 09: Outreach

Leverage Systems Weekend — Sunday, 3:00 PM

Objective

Write one outreach message to one real prospect using your new leverage positioning and proof. Not a template. Not a draft. A real message to a real person that you send before leaving this room.

Duration

90 minutes

Facilitator Guide

Frame (10 min)

"You built a system. You have proof it works. You have positioning. Now you use it. One message. One real prospect. Before you leave today."

This is not a marketing exercise. This is a single, personalized message to someone who could benefit from what you now offer.

Outreach Principles

  1. Specific, not generic. Name the person. Reference their work. Show you know their problem.
  2. Value first, pitch second. Lead with insight or a sample of what you can do, not with "I'd love to chat."
  3. Proof included. Reference or attach your proof — before/after, time savings, sample output.
  4. Clear ask. One specific next step. Not "let me know your thoughts." Something like: "Can I send you a sample analysis of your [specific thing] this week?"
  5. Short. Under 150 words. Busy people don't read long messages.

Build Block (50 min)

Step 1: Select the Prospect (10 min)

Participants identify one real person:

  • Someone they know or have a connection to
  • Someone whose problem matches their positioning
  • Someone who would recognize the value of what they built

Not a cold lead. Not a CEO you've never met. Someone you can reach and who would read your message.

Step 2: Research (10 min)

Spend 10 minutes learning about the prospect:

  • What are they working on right now?
  • What problem do they likely have that your system solves?
  • What language do they use to describe their work?

Step 3: Write the Message (20 min)

Use the template as a starting point, then customize:

  • Open with a specific reference to their work
  • State the value you can provide
  • Include proof (metric, sample, or before/after)
  • Make a clear, specific ask
  • Keep it under 150 words

Step 4: Send (10 min)

Review your message one final time. Then send it. Email, LinkedIn, DM — whatever channel you have access to.

"If you're hesitating, that's normal. Send it anyway. The worst outcome is no response. The best outcome is a client."

Debrief (30 min)

Each participant reads their message to the room (prospect name optional).

  • Facilitator feedback: is the value clear? Is the proof compelling? Is the ask specific?
  • Group discussion: what felt hard about this? What would you change?
  • Reminder: follow up in 3-5 days if no response. One follow-up, not ten.

Pre-Send Checklist

  • Message is under 150 words
  • Opens with something specific to the prospect (not generic)
  • Includes a concrete value statement
  • References proof (metric, sample, or comparison)
  • Ask is specific and easy to say yes to
  • No jargon the prospect wouldn't use
  • Proofread for typos
  • Channel confirmed (you have access)

Post-Send Record

  • Message sent: Yes / No
  • Channel: ___
  • Date/time sent: ___
  • Follow-up scheduled: ___ (3-5 days from send)

Artifact

Each participant: one real outreach message sent to one real prospect.

Weekend Complete

You walked in as someone who uses AI. You're walking out as an operator — with a working leverage system, a value asset, positioning, proof, and your first outreach sent. That's leverage.
