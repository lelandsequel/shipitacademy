Module 01: Kickoff

Leverage Systems Weekend — Saturday, 9:00 AM

Facilitator Guide

Objective

Frame the leverage model. Introduce operator thinking. Every participant leaves Sunday with a complete leverage package — not theory, not a plan, a working system with proof and positioning.

Setup (before participants arrive)

  • Room: power strips, projector, Wi-Fi credentials posted
  • Confirm each participant has access to their tools, accounts, and workspace
  • Print setup checklist as backup

Opening (15 min)

  1. State the mission:

    2. "You are here to build an operator leverage package. By Sunday evening, you'll have a working system that multiplies your output, proof that it works, positioning that explains what you now offer, and one real outreach message sent to a real prospect."

  2. Introduce yourself. Credentials only.
  3. Define "operator" for this weekend:

    4. "An operator is someone who uses AI systems to multiply their output — not as a novelty, but as infrastructure. You don't just use AI. You build leverage with it."

Operator vs. User (10 min)

Draw the distinction:

  • User: asks AI a question, gets an answer, does the work manually
  • Operator: builds a system that takes input, processes it through AI, and produces output at scale
  • Leverage: the ratio between effort invested and output produced. Operators build systems where that ratio compounds.

Participant Introductions (20 min)

Each participant: 60 seconds max.

  • Name
  • What you do (role, industry, clients)
  • Where you spend the most time on repetitive, high-skill work
  • What "10x output" would look like for you

Facilitator: take notes. These introductions reveal the leverage opportunities.

Transition

"Module 02 starts in 5 minutes. You're going to map exactly where your time goes and identify where AI can multiply your output."

Participant Handout

What You're Doing This Weekend

You are building an operator leverage package. By Sunday evening, you will have:

  • A leverage map showing where AI multiplies your output
  • An opportunity thesis for your highest-leverage application
  • A working AI-powered workflow
  • A value asset (doc, template, or tool) that captures your leverage
  • Positioning that explains what you offer and why it's different
  • Proof: before/after comparison showing the leverage
  • One real outreach message sent to one real prospect
